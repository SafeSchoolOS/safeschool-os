Everything Schools Need

Built by educators and security professionals. No per-seat fees. No vendor lock-in.

Emergency Lockdown

One-button lockdown with Alyssa's Law compliance. Sub-5-second SLA, edge-first dispatch, and full audit trail for every drill and live event.

🚶

Visitor Management

Kiosk check-in, watchlist screening, badge printing, host notifications, and overstay detection. Know who's in your building at all times.

🔒

Access Control

Door monitoring, card reader integration, anti-tailgating detection, and zone-based occupancy. Works with Sicunet, Lenel, AMAG, Genetec, and more.

📊

Analytics & Insights

Behavioral drift detection, anomaly alerts, space utilization, and workforce analytics — all from your existing access control data.

📋

Threat Assessment

Structured threat assessment tools, anonymous tip line, case management, and evidence tracking. Proactive safety at every level.

🛡

Incident Response

SOPs, incident lifecycle, responder portal, after-action reports, and dispatch coordination. Prepared for any scenario.

🏫

School Operations

Attendance tracking, hall pass management, transportation, reunification planning, and parent portal — built for K-12.

📱

First Responder Portal

Secure portal for law enforcement with floor plans, live door control, camera feeds, and pre-incident data packages.

Cloud + Edge Hybrid

Runs on a Mini PC at your school with cloud sync. Works offline during emergencies. Fleet management for districts from one dashboard.
